About Me

I lead social media and community strategy for Microsoft Intune and Microsoft Security within Customer Experience Engineering (CxE). My work connects engineering teams with the global technical community by turning real customer conversations into actionable insights, technical guidance, and engagement that actually helps people get their work done.

With deep roots at Microsoft, I focus on bringing customers, engineers, and community voices together to improve how products evolve and how people experience them. My role often sits between teams—translating feedback, identifying patterns, and helping shape solutions that scale.

Before Microsoft, I worked my way up from systems administrator in Hawai'i through desktop support and service technician roles. That hands-on background gives me a practical perspective on the customer journey—from the person racking the server to the person deploying the endpoint.
